The Short Version

Why I Wrote This Book.

I've interviewed thousands of salespeople. I've made the offers. I've also watched good candidates get passed over for the wrong reasons — and the wrong candidates get hired because they had the right answer to the wrong question.

For every candidate who walks into a room confused about what's actually being measured, there's a hiring manager on the other side of that table who doesn't fully know either. That's the gap. The book is the bridge.

I named it Now Hiring Salespeople because that's the sign every candidate has read — and almost none of them have been told what's behind it.

I wrote it to give back to a profession that has given me so much. Young people deserve a fighting chance to get started — when they're willing to put in the work.
