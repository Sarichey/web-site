The Problem

They Say "Entry Level."
They Expect a Pro.

You've done everything right. You updated your resume. You researched the company. You practiced your answers. And you still didn't get the call back.

Here's what no one told you: sales hiring doesn't work the way you think it does. The person sitting across from you isn't just evaluating your answers. They're filtering for specific traits through a very specific lens — and that lens changes depending on who they are.

No one teaches you how sales hiring actually works. This book does.

Framework 01

The 5 Hiring Manager Personas

Every interviewer falls into one of five patterns. Read the pattern, match the message.

  • Developer — The Coach
  • Builder — Player / Coach
  • Operator — Process Manager
  • Closer — Results First
  • Pressure — Under the Gun

Framework 02

The Decoder Framework

The five-step pattern for handling any hard question — without freezing.

  • D — Decode the question
  • R — Reframe the angle
  • A — Answer with structure
  • P — Prove with a story
  • C — Close the loop
