For Career Services

Your Students Are Walking Into a Test They've Never Studied For.

Sales is the most likely first job a graduate will hold. It's also the role they're least prepared for. Resume reviews and mock interviews don't move the needle when the person on the other side of the table is filtering for traits no one taught your students to recognize.

The campus program from Now Hiring Salespeople gives your students the map. Real questions. Real personas. The same playbook hiring managers already use — handed back to the people they're interviewing.

Think of it as handing your students the answer key — to a test most candidates never see coming.

What changes

Higher placement rates. Your students learn to close the interview the same way they'll one day close a deal. More yes's, more offers, better placement numbers.

Stronger employer pipelines. Hiring managers remember which schools send prepared candidates — and call yours back first.

Better retention. Grads who walked in prepared stay longer. Your post-graduation outcomes stop getting hammered by churn at month six.
